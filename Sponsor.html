ISACA is an independent, nonprofit, and global association. ISACA engages in the development, globally accepted, and industry-leading knowledge and practices for information systems. Isaca was previously known as the Information Systems Audit and Control Association, but was changed to the acronym to reflect the broad range of IT governance professionals it serves. ISACA formed the ITGI to focus on original research, publications, resources and symposia on IT governance and related topics. In addition to the work carried out by the ITGI. ISACA also addresses the topic through conference sessions and tracks and also education courses. ISACA offers different certificated including CISA (Certified Information Systems Audtior), CISM (Certified Information System Manager), CGEIT (Certified in the Governance of Enterprise IT), and the CRISC (Certified in Risk and Information Systems Control).
